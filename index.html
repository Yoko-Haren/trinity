About the Project

BSIT Thesis · Davao del Norte State College

This research presents a web-based automated school form system with AI-powered anomaly detection for public secondary schools in Davao del Norte Division. The system addresses challenges in managing DepEd School Forms (SF1-SF10)—currently handled through error-prone manual Excel processes. By centralizing records, automating form generation, and implementing AI monitoring, Formify reduces teacher workload, enhances data integrity, and supports DepEd's digital transformation.
